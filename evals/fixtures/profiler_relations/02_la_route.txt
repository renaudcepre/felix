La Route des Cretes
Moyen-Age fantastique
1247-06-22
Sentier des Cretes

Borin and Elara walk along the Sentier des Cretes. The wind is glacial. They advance in silence, like two traveling companions who have no need for words.

They come across a group of merchants. Among them, Darya, an itinerant herbalist. She walks with the merchants, sharing their road and their campfire. Darya carries a bag of medicinal plants on her back.

DARYA
You're heading to the pass? It's dangerous this time of year.

BORIN
We know. Thanks for the advice.

Darya offers them an ointment against the cold. Borin accepts with a gruff gesture. Elara thanks her with a nod.

The group parts ways at the fork in the path. The merchants take the southern road. Borin and Elara continue toward the pass.

ELARA
She had the accent of the marsh folk.

BORIN
Herbalists go everywhere. Don't read into accents.

They make camp at nightfall. Borin prepares the fire. Elara stands guard, bow in hand. Their mentor-and-apprentice bond needs no speeches.
