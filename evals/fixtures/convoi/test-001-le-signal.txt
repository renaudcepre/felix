2061 : Le Signal (Lena Voss)

The communication terminal blinked in the silence of the Helios-3 orbital relay station. Lena Voss, transmission engineer, stared at the screen where an unknown sequence had been repeating on loop for exactly fourteen minutes.

— This isn't background noise, she murmured, adjusting the filters. It's structured. It's intentional.

Her assistant, a class 6 drone nicknamed "Pixel," projected the spectral analysis onto the porthole glass. The sequence originated from beyond the asteroid belt. No known human equipment was operating in that zone.

Lena hesitated, her fingers hovering above the transmission button to Earth Command. If she was right, everything changed. If she was wrong, she would lose her post for a false alarm.

She pressed it.
