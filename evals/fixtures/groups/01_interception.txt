2061 : L'Interception (Lena Voss, Pixel)

In the control room of Helios-3, Lena Voss monitored the security screens. A maintenance drone squadron — which the crew simply called "the drones" — was making regular passes along the outer hull.

Pixel, her class 6 AI assistant, detected an anomaly in sector C.

— Abnormal energy flux, sector C-7, he said in his calm synthetic voice.

Lena sat up straight. She had read the reports: raiders always struck in groups, never alone. Someone had cut the external relays.

— Pixel, contact command. And tell the drones to reposition toward sector C.

Pixel transmitted the order. The drones redirected immediately. In the adjacent corridor, the on-duty guards took up positions at the security airlocks.

Lena stared at the screen. If the raiders attacked now, the drone squadron would be their first line of defense.
