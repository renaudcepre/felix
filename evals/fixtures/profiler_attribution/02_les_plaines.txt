La Traversée des Plaines
Années 3000
3001-03-17
Plaines de Sel

Gandalf, Aldric, and Mira cross the Plaines de Sel under a grey sky. The wind blows hard, bending the dry grass.

Mira is an experienced archer from the city of Vorn. She walks as a scout, bow in hand.

MIRA
Something is wrong. The birds have gone silent.

Gandalf stops and closes his eyes. A long silence.

GANDALF
We are being followed. Not by Nazgûl, not yet. Orc scouts.

ALDRIC
How many?

GANDALF
About ten. They're closing in from the eastern flank.

Aldric draws his sword and takes position to the right of Gandalf. Mira turns around and draws her bow.

MIRA
I see them. Eight, not ten.

A brief fight breaks out. Gandalf strikes two orcs with his staff with devastating precision. Aldric neutralizes three with his sword. Mira takes down the others from a distance.

The group resumes its march in silence. Aldric picks up a broken arrow from the ground and examines it.

ALDRIC
They bore the mark of Dûrash. The lord of the Marshes has sold us out.

GANDALF
We should have expected as much. Let us change course toward the collines de Narath.
