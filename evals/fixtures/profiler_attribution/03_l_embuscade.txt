L'Embuscade de Narath
Années 3000
3001-03-19
Collines de Narath

Night falls over the collines de Narath. Gandalf, Aldric, and Mira advance in silence along a narrow path.

Suddenly, a dark figure emerges from the rocks. A Nazgûl. Gandalf raises his staff and projects a burst of white light. The Nazgûl retreats, but a second figure appears behind Aldric.

MIRA
Aldric! Behind you!

Before Aldric can turn around, the Nazgûl drives a Morgul blade into his right shoulder. Aldric collapses with a cry. The Morgul blade is a black, ice-cold steel forged in the furnaces of Minas Morgul.

Gandalf pivots and repels the second Nazgûl with a discharge of light. Mira rushes to Aldric and presses her hand against the wound.

MIRA
The blade is still in the wound. We need to remove it now.

GANDALF
Don't move. I'll get the healing herb from my bag.

Gandalf searches through his belongings. His face is grave but he is not wounded. Aldric loses consciousness, his complexion ashen, the cold of the Morgul blade spreading through his blood.

GANDALF
Hold his hand. I must recite the incantation before the poison reaches his heart.

Mira grips Aldric's hand. Gandalf kneels beside him and begins to murmur ancient words. Aldric's wound is deep, his life in danger from the cursed blade.
