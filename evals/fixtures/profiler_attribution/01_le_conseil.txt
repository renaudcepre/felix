Le Conseil des Ombres
Années 3000
3001-03-15
Citadelle de Miren

Gandalf arrives at the citadelle de Miren at nightfall. Aldric awaits him in the council hall, surrounded by maps and scrolls.

Gandalf leans his staff against the wall and sits across from Aldric.

GANDALF
The Nazgûl are closing in. I tracked them from the col de Kharak. They're searching for the bearer.

ALDRIC
How much time do we have?

GANDALF
Three days, maybe four. No more.

Aldric stands and examines the map. He is a young warrior with black hair, wearing the armor of the Miren guard. He carries a short sword at his belt.

ALDRIC
I know a passage through the Plaines de Sel. Slower, but the Nazgûl avoid that place.

GANDALF
Good. We leave at dawn. You, me, and Mira if she agrees to join us.

Gandalf reopens his scrolls and begins tracing a route. His face is focused, his grey eyes scanning the maps with the precision of an old traveler.
