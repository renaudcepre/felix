STATION HELIOS-3 — COMMAND CENTER — MARCH 13, 2157

Commander Chen Wei listens to Irina's report without interrupting.

"Are you sure it's not a calibration artifact?" he asks at last.

"Positive. I ran the verification three times." Irina sets her terminal down on
the table. "The signal matches a propulsion signature I've never seen
in the archives."

Chen Wei stands up. "I'm notifying the Nairobi control center. You and Kofi,
keep monitoring. Nobody else knows about this for now."

Irina nods. In fifteen years working in space observation, she had
never seen Chen Wei lose his composure — and right now, he was clearly worried.
