STATION HELIOS-3 — CONTROL ROOM — MARCH 12, 2157

Irina Voss looks up from her terminal. The emission data from satellite DR-7
no longer matches nominal parameters: a 3.2 Hz frequency anomaly
appeared at 04:17 UT.

"Kofi, did you see this?" she calls out to her colleague.

Kofi Adu walks over, fingers flying across the keyboard. "Unregistered signal.
Looks like a surface echo... but the source is at 400 kilometers altitude."

Irina logs the time in her mission journal and triggers the alert protocol.
It is the first time in eighteen months that an unidentified signal has passed through
the station's filters.
