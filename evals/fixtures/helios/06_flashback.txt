STATION HELIOS-3 — CONTROL ROOM — MARCH 16, 2157

Irina Voss stares at the signal data without seeing it.

She remembers. She was still at station Kepler-9, two years before being
assigned to Helios-3. A similar anomaly had appeared — a 2.8 Hz parasitic signal,
quickly classified as atmospheric noise. She had signed the closing report
without pushing further.

"Irina?" Kofi Adu places a hand on her shoulder. "You okay?"

She snaps back to the present. "At Kepler-9, two years ago, I saw something similar.
I let it go. I shouldn't have."

Kofi sits down across from her. "You think it was the same signal?"

"I think we're about to make the same mistake."
