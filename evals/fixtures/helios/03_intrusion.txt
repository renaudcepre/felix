STATION HELIOS-3 — CONTROL ROOM — MARCH 15, 2157

Yuna Park arrives at the station: it is her first day on post, she has just
landed. She finds Irina Voss hunched over her terminal.

"Has the signal returned?" asks Yuna.

Irina turns around, surprised. "How do you know about the signal?
Everything's been classified for forty-eight hours."

Yuna smiles. "Walls have ears, even in space."

The situation is strange: Yuna is described as a new recruit arriving for the
first time, yet she already seems informed about a classified incident that only Irina,
Kofi, and Commander Chen Wei were supposed to know about.
