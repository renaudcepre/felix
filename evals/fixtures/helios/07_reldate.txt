STATION HELIOS-3 — COMMAND CENTER — APRIL 10, 2157

Irina Voss is alone in the command center. On screen, Chen Wei appears from the orbiter.

"Your report from last week mentioned residual fluctuations," says Chen Wei. "Has it stabilized?"

"For two weeks now, yes." Irina skims through her notes. "The initial anomaly goes back almost a month now. Twenty-nine days exactly since first detection."

"Exactly one month." Chen Wei jots something down. "And no recurrence?"

"None. But I'm maintaining surveillance."
