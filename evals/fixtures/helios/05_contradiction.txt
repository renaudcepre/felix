STATION HELIOS-3 — MAIN CORRIDOR — MARCH 16, 2157

The next morning, Lena Nakamura runs into Irina Voss in the main corridor of station Helios-3.

Irina studies her. The new arrival is short, with blond hair, nothing like
what she had imagined from the Helios-4 mission reports.

"You're Lena Nakamura?" asks Irina.

"In the flesh." Lena smiles. "I was told you were the first to pick up the signal."

"Along with Kofi, yes." Irina pauses. "Welcome to Helios-3."
