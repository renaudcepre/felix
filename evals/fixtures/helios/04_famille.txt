STATION HELIOS-3 — BREAK ROOM — MARCH 15, 2157

Hana Nakamura is adjusting a sensor panel when her sister walks into the room.

Lena Nakamura sets down her bag. Tall, with short-cropped black hair, she has just arrived from Helios-4, where she was stationed for two years.

"Dad tried to reach you three times this week," says Lena. "Kenji Nakamura is starting to worry."

"I know." Hana closes the panel. "I've been swamped since the signal incident. Tell him I'm fine."

"Tell him yourself." Lena sits down. "It's been years since the two of us were at the same station at the same time."

Hana smiles for the first time in days.
