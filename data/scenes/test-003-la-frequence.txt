2061: La Frequence (Milton, Voss)

In the basement of the Neo-Santiago depot, Milton listened to the intercepted message for the third time. The transmission, picked up by a pirate relay, came from an orbital station — probably Helios.

— It's gibberish, he said, removing his headset. Just cosmic noise.

Voss shook her head. She laid a spectral diagram on the rusted metal table.

— Look at the periodicity. Every 7.3 seconds, exactly. Cosmic noise doesn't have periodicity. It's the same signal I picked up in orbit.

Milton leaned over the diagram. He recognized the pattern — something similar to the old modulation codes the megacorporation used for its encrypted communications.

— If it's them, he murmured, then they're talking to someone. And that someone isn't on Earth.
